1]Compare the time complexity of linear and binary search algorithms. 
Linear Search:
Checks one element at a time.
Time Complexity:
Best: O(1)
Average: O(n)
Worst: O(n)

Binary Search
Requires sorted array.
Divides search space into half repeatedly.
Time Complexity:
Best: O(1)
Average: O(log n)
Worst: O(log n)
